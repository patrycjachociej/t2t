Judo

MMA or Mixed Martial Arts is the fusion of many existing martial arts into one combative style.
It is also many thousands of years old with its first inception into the Ancient Greek Olympics in the 648 BC. Utilizing various codes of wrestling, Brazilian Jujitsu, Judo, Karate, Boxing, Muay Thai, Tae Kwondo as it primary though not exclusive martial arts sources MMA is the most modern up to date from of weaponless self defense and the worlds most popular combat sport.
